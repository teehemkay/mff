European elections 2024 6-9 June 2024

How to vote in Austria

(opens in the same window on selection)

This section will provide you with information regarding the elections, voting procedures and deadlines for registration if you are an EU citizen and live in another EU Member State or outside the European Union and you would like to vote for your country of nationality in the European elections (if your country of nationality allows it) or if you are an EU citizen and reside in an EU Member State of which you are not a national and you want to vote for candidates in your EU Member State of residence.

  • It is up to each country to manage the election, but there are some common principles they must apply.

    • Elections take place during a four-day period, from Thursday to Sunday.
    • The number of MEPs elected from a political party is proportional to the number of votes it receives.
    • EU citizens resident in another EU country can vote and stand for election there
    • Each citizen can vote only once.

    Read more about the common and national rules. Dedicated pages with more information about the European elections in your country will be available soon.
